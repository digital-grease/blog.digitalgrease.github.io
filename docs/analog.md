# Analog

Blacksmithing, farming, building things with your hands. Walipini greenhouses, cedar enclosures, sheep and chickens. The work that happens after the screen goes dark.

Hot metal and code are more alike than people think. Both do exactly what you tell them, not what you mean. This is half of the work; the [other half](/digital/) lives in terminals and threat models.
